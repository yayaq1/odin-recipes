Chocolate Mousse

Description

Chocolate mousse is a light and fluffy dessert that is made with whipped cream, egg whites, and chocolate. It is a classic French dessert that is often served at the end of a meal. It is also a popular dessert for special occasions, such as Valentine’s Day and anniversaries.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate
  • Whipped Cream
  • Egg Whites


Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate
  2. Whip the cream
  3. Whip the egg whites
  4. Combine the chocolate and cream
  5. Combine the egg whites and chocolate mixture
  6. Chill the mousse
  7. Enjoy your meal
